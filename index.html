About Local SEO

Local SEO focuses on optimizing a business’s online presence to attract customers from specific geographic areas.

Google Business Profile plays a critical role in local visibility, appearing in map packs and local search results.

NAP consistency (Name, Address, Phone) ensures accurate business information across directories.

Local citations and schema markup strengthen trust signals and improve search engine understanding.

Multi-location and service area strategies help scale local SEO efforts for businesses operating in multiple regions.
